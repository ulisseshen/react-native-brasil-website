## Translation Instructions

### Debugging Documentation Guidelines

1. **Debug Tools** - Keep tool names in English:
   - React DevTools, Chrome DevTools, Flipper
   - Debug menu options may be kept in English
   - Commands and shortcuts unchanged

2. **Console Methods** - Keep unchanged:
   - `console.log()`, `console.warn()`, `console.error()`
   - Debugger statements: `debugger`, `__DEV__`

3. **Error Messages** - Translate descriptions:
   - Keep error codes/names in English
   - Translate explanations and solutions

### UI/Styling Guidelines

1. **CSS/Flexbox Terms** - Mostly keep in English:
   - `flexDirection`, `justifyContent`, `alignItems`
   - `flex`, `flexWrap`, `flexGrow`, `flexShrink`
   - Values: `'row'`, `'column'`, `'center'`, `'space-between'`

2. **Accessibility** - Mixed approach:
   - Keep ARIA properties in English: `accessibilityLabel`, `accessibilityHint`
   - Translate accessibility concepts and best practices
